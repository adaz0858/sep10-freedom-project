Financial Services

Context

Financial services distribute wealth between people and collect taxes. We will be looking at past innovations used for financial services to brainstorm for a new innovation for the future.There are lots of softwares and hardwares that are used in the financial world. We have used these past innovations to come up with new and more advanced ways to balance our incomes. These innovations are also advanced over time in order to provide customers with various ways to balance their income.

Existing Software

YNAB:

Good for paying off debts. It helps you keep track of how much you earned and when you earned your money. They make you spend money you earned at least 30 days ago and the average amount of time it takes for you to spend the money you earned.
Cost:34 day free trial, $14.99 a month,and $98.99 a year.

MINT:

Helps you keep track of your spendings, however ads might be annoying for free users. Mint is one of the best apps with free usage and low premium and no ad cost.
Cost: Completely free unless you want no ads then it’ll cost $0.99 per month and $4.99 per month for premium.

ACORNS:

Beginner friendly investing app that helps invest money you've spent by rounding.
Example: You buy something for $12.50, they invest the $0.50 for you.
Cost:$3 per month personal plan and $5 per month family plan.

Existing Hardware

Mainframe:

Mainframes are able to process thousands of transactions per second. It has a capacity that can manage terabytes of information. Many businesses use mainframes in order to perform these properties.
Cost: A single mainframe can cost up to 75,000 US dollars. The prices can range up to millions of dollars.

Router:

Routers are necessary if you want to connect multiple devices together. It allows data to transfer quicker.
Cost: Routers typically cost around $100 dollars but price varies as the efficiency of the router varies.

Calculator:

These financial calculators are able to calculate basic equations and time value of money. They can also calculate interest rates, payments and present and future values of money.
Cost: Financial calculators are usually priced around $30 dollars.
